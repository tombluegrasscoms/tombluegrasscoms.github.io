F.A.Q

If you've got a question about our Company, you may find the information you are after here. Answers to the questions we are most frequently asked.



FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS  



F.A.Q


Q.  My understanding is that the VISION system is designed to speed up the TLA process but what kind of lead times can we expect?

A.  VISION has a rule built within the programme that can highlight potential TLA instantly meaning a representative from Bluegrass will contact you within 15 minutes of exporting. At this point they will ask if you require any assistance to save the vehicle. Once your estimator has confirmed that he requires the additional discount Bluegrass will confirm all the correct part numbers, calculate the offer and email you with 20- 30 minutes. In short the entire process should take no longer than 35-45 minutes.

Q.  Do Bluegrasscoms represent any other car manufacturers, if so how do we sign up to benefit from them?

A.  As well as Vauxhall, Bluegrasscoms are currently working on behalf of Peugeot, Citroen and Renault. They have recently announced that as well as Total loss avoidance, PSA are offering an O.E price matching process meaning they are actively discounting parts to compete with the aftermarket via the VISION system. All bodyshops are eligible but must register, to do so simply contact them on 0844 247 3577.

Q.  I am lead to believe that Bluegrass receives the parts list at the point of export however we are currently not in the habit of exporting, is there another way for VISION to identify a TLA?

A.  Unfortunately not. VISION is installed on the export path of your estimating PC meaning that in order for Bluegrass to see the estimate and identify a TLA you must click the export button. We do however remind all bodyshops that there is no charge for this step nor will it slow down any of your PC’s. VISION also removes the process of copying and pasting your parts list into an email that in the past was sent to Jason. We also remind you that by clicking export Bluegrass will be able to contact your estimator within 15 minutes.

Q.  If we change our Estimators PC do you need to reinstall VISION?

A.  The answer does depend on whether VISION was originally installed on individual PC’s or a server but to be absolutely sure Bluegrass do ask you to contact them in all instances so they can confirm that the system is still active.

Q.  Do I have to change my current parts dealer?

A.  No, When you had VISION installed you were asked to nominate which dealer you would like your parts to be ordered from which is saved within the system. The only way this can be changed is if you say so.
