CAREERS

If you are looking to make a difference in an amazing team and are interested in personal development, Vision is the career choice for you. We are looking for individuals that are smart, ambitious, empathetic, and work with in a lively team environment. Previous experience in the automotive and parts industry is desired but not essential. Here at Bluegrass, we are always on the lookout for talented and motivated individuals.
