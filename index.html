TOTAL LOSS AVOIDANCE

VISION is the only system on the market to offer efficient, accurate and instant decisions for vehicles deemed to be written off by the insurers. Every year, thousands of vehicles are written off by insurance companies because their repair is deemed too expensive relative to the vehicle’s market value. For this reason we have created VISION.

Used by leading vehicle manufactures VISION automatically uses pre-set algorithms to populate a suggested discount to save vehicles which may have otherwise resulted in a total loss. This discount goes a long way to reducing the overall cost of the repair, saving the vehicle from write-off and keeping the vehicle on the road. VISION is the only system on the market to offer efficient, accurate and instant decisions for vehicles deemed to be written off by the insurers.
